# Ad Brief Template

For paid ads on Google, Meta, LinkedIn, or other platforms.

```markdown
---
title: [Ad Campaign Name]
type: ad
date: YYYY-MM-DD
platform: [google|meta|linkedin|other]
---

# [Ad Campaign Name]

## Campaign Objective
[Awareness / Traffic / Lead generation / Conversion / Retargeting]

## Target Audience

**Platform audience targeting:**
[Demographics, interests, behaviors, job titles, company size — whatever the platform supports]

**Where they are in the funnel:**
[Cold / Warm retargeting / Hot intent]

## Core Message
[Single sentence — what we want them to feel or know]

## Platform Specifications

### Google (if applicable)
- **Campaign type:** [Search / Display / PMax / YouTube]
- **Target keywords:** [primary keywords]
- **Match type:** [Broad / Phrase / Exact]

### Meta (if applicable)
- **Placement:** [Feed / Stories / Reels / Audience Network]
- **Image specs:** [1080x1080 or 1080x1920]
- **Video length:** [if applicable]

### LinkedIn (if applicable)
- **Ad format:** [Single Image / Carousel / Video / Document / InMail]
- **Targeting:** [Job title, function, seniority, company size]

## Creative Brief

### Headline Options (provide 3-5)
1.
2.
3.

### Body Copy (primary text)
[The main ad copy — what appears in the body of the ad]

### Call to Action
**CTA button:** [Learn More / Get Started / Download / Sign Up]
**Destination URL:** [URL with UTM parameters]

## A/B Test Hypothesis
[What are we testing and what do we expect to learn?]

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Goal |
|--------|------|
| CTR | [target] |
| CPC | [max] |
| CPA | [max] |
| ROAS | [target, if e-commerce] |

## Budget
- **Daily budget:** $[amount]
- **Total campaign budget:** $[amount]
- **Flight dates:** [start] → [end]
```
