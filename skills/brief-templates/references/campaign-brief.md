# [Campaign Title]

## Campaign Objective
[SMART objective — specific, measurable, actionable, realistic, time-bound]

## Target Audience

**Who they are:**
[Specific person description — role, company type, company size]

**What they care about:**
[Their goals, fears, and daily challenges — in their language]

**Where they are in the buying journey:**
[Aware of problem / aware of solution / comparing options / ready to buy]

## Core Message
[Single sentence — the one thing they must remember]

## Supporting Proof Points
1. [Reason to believe #1 — factual, specific]
