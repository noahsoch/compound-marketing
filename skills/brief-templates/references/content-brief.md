# Content Brief Template

For blog posts, articles, newsletters, long-form guides, and case studies.

```markdown
---
title: [Content Title]
type: content
date: YYYY-MM-DD
format: [blog|newsletter|guide|case-study|video-script]
---

# [Working Title]

## Goal
[What this piece is supposed to do — drive SEO traffic, nurture leads, build authority, etc.]

## Target Reader

**Who they are:**
[Specific person — role, experience level, context they're reading in]

**What problem brings them here:**
[The question or problem that made them search for or open this]

**What they should leave with:**
[One clear takeaway or next step]

## Primary Keyword (if SEO)
[Target keyword phrase]
**Search intent:** [Informational / Commercial / Transactional]
**Monthly searches:** [Rough volume if known]

## Core Argument
[The main point in one sentence — the thesis]

## Outline

### Introduction
[How to hook the reader — lead with the tension, problem, or surprising claim]

### Section 1: [Heading]
[Key point]

### Section 2: [Heading]
[Key point]

### Section 3: [Heading]
[Key point]

### Conclusion
[Wrap up and transition to CTA]

## Call to Action
**Action:** [What we want the reader to do next]
**CTA copy:** [Exact link or button text]
**Destination:** [URL]

## Tone
[3 adjectives or reference example]

## Specs
- **Target word count:** [range]
- **Target reading time:** [X min]
- **Images needed:** [number and type]

## References and Sources
[Links to research, data, or examples to include]
```
